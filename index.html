Our Story

A Legacy of Culinary Excellence

Born from Passion, Crafted with Precision

Founded in 2018 in the heart of San Francisco's North Beach, Bella Vista was born from Executive Chef Alessandro Marchetti's vision to bring authentic Italian fine dining to the West Coast — without compromise.

Every dish tells a story of heritage and innovation. We source the finest ingredients from local farms and Italian importers, honoring tradition while embracing contemporary technique.

Our 2,400-bottle wine cellar features rare Barolos, Super Tuscans, and hidden gems curated by our award-winning sommelier, Isabella Fontana.
